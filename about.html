Years of Experience

Dr. Sumeetkaur Mehta Patil

Gynecologist Laparoscopy & Infertility Specialist Specialist

She has 16+ years of experience in the field of gynecology. She has done her MBBS from Vasantrao Pawar Medical College. After that, she attained her DNB (Ob/Gy) from the renowned center, Masina Hospital under the guidance of Dr. Ameya Purandare and Dr. FR Soonawaala. She has done her 1-year reproductive medicine infertility fellowship from the renowned institute, CIMAR, under the famous DR. K. K. Gopinath. She has also done her 1-year fellowship in Laparoscopy at Paul's Hospital, Kerala under the pioneer and world-famous surgeon DR. P. G. Paul. She is trained and has been doing all the basic and complicated laparoscopy surgeries. She has been active in research & has various national and international publications.
